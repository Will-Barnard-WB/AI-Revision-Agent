# Linear Algebra Revision

## Key Topics

1. **Vectors**  
   - Definition and properties  
   - Operations: addition, scalar multiplication  
   - Dot product and cross product  

2. **Matrices**  
   - Definition and types (square, diagonal, identity)  
   - Matrix operations: addition, multiplication, inversion  
   - Determinants and their properties  

3. **Linear Transformations**  
   - Definition and examples  
   - Matrix representation of linear transformations  
   - Kernel and range  

4. **Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors**  
   - Definition and significance  
   - Characteristic polynomial  
   - Applications in systems of differential equations  

5. **Vector Spaces**  
   - Definition and examples  
   - Subspaces, basis, and dimension  
   - Linear independence and span  

6. **Systems of Linear Equations**  
   - Representation in matrix form  
   - Gaussian elimination and row echelon form  
   - Homogeneous and non-homogeneous systems  

7. **Inner Product Spaces**  
   - Definition and properties  
   - Orthogonality and orthonormal sets  
   - Gram-Schmidt process
